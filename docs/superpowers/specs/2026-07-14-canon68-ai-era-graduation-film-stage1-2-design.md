## 1. Goal

Turn the approved discussion direction into a real, executable nine-minute
graduation-film script before any new selection or rendering occurs. The film
must feel authored rather than catalogued: each section advances one collective
transformation, and every future material choice must prove a beat instead of
merely filling time.

The restrained thesis is:

> 時代不斷向前，而每一度穩定的電，都從有人願意學會承擔開始。

AI is historical context and a production footnote, not the protagonist and
not a claim that synthetic imagery proves training. The protagonists are the
trainees, instructors, supervisors, and support staff whose repeated actions
turn uncertainty into readiness.

## 2. Superseded Creative Route

The rejected Canon 67 150-second category arrangement remains negative

- category labels did not create a story;
- adjacent-only duplicate checks missed delayed repetition;
- unrelated categories were interleaved without action progression;
- filler images occupied time without advancing a beat;
- still motion ignored the visible person/action;

This design replaces that arrangement as the active creative route. It does
not delete the old candidate or its evidence.

Canon 67 raw material may now be inspected as same-family proxy evidence to

## 3. Narrative Approach

The film uses a collective-protagonist transformation rather than a course
catalog. Its narrative device is the contrast between a world that keeps
accelerating and people learning to become steady enough to support it.

```text
the world moves faster
→ strangers enter one discipline
→ repeated actions become coordinated skill
→ pressure reveals trust
→ real voices name what has changed
→ gratitude and departure convert training into responsibility
```

The AI-era idea appears only in the opening/closing thesis and, if material
truth supports it, a short non-proof visual bridge. It must not introduce
crisis rhetoric, political claims about electricity supply, futuristic horror,
or invented first-person feelings for the trainees.

## 5. Picture Grammar

Every technical beat is a micro-story:

```text
establish task → prepare → attempt → coordinate/correct → visible result
```

Related actions stay together long enough to develop meaning. A bridge image
is legal only when it connects cause, location, time, or emotion; “nice but
unnecessary” material is not filler. Repetition review covers adjacent clips,
a sliding semantic window, the whole segment, and the whole film.

Still motion must start from an observed subject/action anchor. The default is
face, hands, tool contact, line of force, or group movement—not the geometric
center and never empty sky unless the sky is itself story evidence. When no
reliable anchor can be identified, use a stable hold or a restrained whole-frame
push instead of pretending to know.

## 6. Audio, Text, And Effects

- Source speech has priority over music. Interview sections preserve complete
  sentences and use automatic ducking only after transcript approval.
- Music supports act-level energy; it does not run at one loudness under speech.
- Review proxies carry draft chapter/beat captions so the owner can understand
  intended structure. These are review aids, not approved final copy.
- Subtitles rendered from speech must equal owner-approved transcript text.
- Effects are restrained: one opening thesis family, consistent chapter cards,
  readable lower thirds, and motivated transitions. No effect may obscure proof.
- Generated visuals are optional, support-only, clearly declared, and forbidden
  as proof of a person, cohort, event, skill, or institutional fact.
- Material Map may propose a generated bridge only when the corresponding need
  explicitly has `generated_allowed=true` and `proof_type=support_only`.
  Photorealistic output remains generated support; it never becomes documentary
  evidence because it looks real.

## 7. Human And Machine Boundaries

The agent may author the Stage 1–2 proposal, assign story functions, and expose
material gaps. The owner must approve the story blueprint, the director shot
plan, all final text, picture lock, and delivery. L0 will later test whether raw
material actually covers these needs; missing proof causes rewrite, shortening,
collection, or explicit waiver—not semantic relabeling.

The generated-material fallback is therefore asymmetric: missing bridges may
be generated and reviewed, while missing proof must return to collection or
story revision. Every generated bridge must preserve provider/prompt/reference,
model, file hash, import lineage, and human review evidence before it may be
accepted into Material Map.

This package stops at `WAITING_OWNER_STAGE1_2_VERDICT`. It creates no candidate,
timeline, music download, generated media, worker dispatch, or delivery claim.
