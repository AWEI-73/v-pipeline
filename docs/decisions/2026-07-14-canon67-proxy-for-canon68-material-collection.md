# Decision: Canon 67 Is Proxy Evidence For Canon 68 Collection Design

Date: 2026-07-14  
Status: ACCEPTED BY OWNER  
Scope: Canon 68 AI-era graduation film, Stage 3 / L0

## Decision

Use the available Canon 67 material family as a proxy case study to determine
what Canon 68 trainees must record and submit. Canon 67 and Canon 68 share the
same event/training family, so Canon 67 can expose likely coverage, continuity,
and evidence gaps.

Canon 67 material is not Canon 68 factual proof. It must never satisfy Canon 68
identity, roster, speech, attendance, event, or delivery claims. No Canon 67
person may be labeled as Canon 68.

## L0 Success Criterion

For every Canon 68 `material_need`, L0 records:

1. whether Canon 67 proxy evidence shows the requested shot family is practical;
2. the evidence reference and its limits;
3. what Canon 68 must submit to create direct story evidence;
4. minimum continuity, shot-scale, audio, identity, and metadata requirements;
5. whether the future Canon 68 need remains mandatory, optional, or requires
   owner-supplied factual text.

The output is a collection/submission brief, not selects, picture lock, BUILD
handoff, or a render candidate.

## Boundaries

- Existing Canon 67 candidate videos remain negative calibration or proxy
  review evidence only.
- The Canon 67 final film and Canon 66 materials remain excluded as source
  material.
- Proxy coverage cannot change `human_creative_approval` or
  `final_delivery_claimed`.
- Canon 68 factual coverage stays UNKNOWN until its own raw assets, hashes,
  transcripts, roster, and owner review exist.

## Generated Bridge Boundary

Material Map may route a missing or thin Canon 68 need to generated material
only when the need explicitly declares `generated_allowed=true` and its
`proof_type` is `support_only`. This is a bridge fallback, not factual repair.

Generated support may connect time, place, chapter, or emotion. It must enter
Material Map as a reviewed candidate and carry generated provenance. It cannot
prove a real person, identity, cohort, event, attendance, skill, safety action,
technical result, speech, roster, mentorship, backstage care, or departure.

When a proof need is missing, the legal responses are collect/reshoot, choose
another real event with a complete evidence chain, rewrite, shorten, or omit
with owner approval. Photorealism changes visual quality, not truth status.
